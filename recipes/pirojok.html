Pirojok

Central Asian dish with lamb and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Lamb
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook whole potatoes on olive oil until golden brown
  2. Take potatoes out of kazan
  3. Put pre-cut lamb to kazan and cook for 30 minutes
  4. Add potatoes back to kazan and close the lid. Put the heat to low and simmer for 1 hour
